## Formulas From Grasple and Other places

* $$sin(arctan(x)) = \frac{x}{\sqrt{1 + x^2}}$$
* $$cos(arctan(x)) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 + x^2}}$$
* $$f(x) = \frac{ax + b}{cx + d} \implies f^{-1}(x) = \frac{-dx + b}{cx - a}$$
