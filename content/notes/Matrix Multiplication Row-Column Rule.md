When you [[/notes/Matrix Multiplication|multiply]] two matrices, $A$ and $B$, and only want one entry($ij$) in the result matrice $C$, we can use the formula:

$$
C_{ij} = A_{i1}B_{1j} + A_{i2}B_{2j} + A_{i3}B_{3j} + ... + A_{in}B_{nj}
$$
