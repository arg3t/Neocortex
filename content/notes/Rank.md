The rank of a [[notes/Matrixes|matrix]] $A$, denoted by $Rank(A)$, is the [[notes/Dimension|Dimension]] of the [[notes/Column Space|Column Space]] of $A$.

> If $A$ is a matrix of size $m \times n$, then $Rank(A) + dim(Nul(A)) = n$
