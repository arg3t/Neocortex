Given the [[notes/Vectors|Vectors]] $v_1,v_2,...v_n$ in $\mathbb{R}^n$, and scalars $c_1,c_2,...,c_n$, the linear combination of these vectors, $y$, is:

$$
y = c_1v_1 + c_2v_2 + c_3v_3 + ... + c_nv_n
$$

where the scalars are called the **weights**.
