The product of a matrix $A$ and a [[/notes/Vectors|vector]] $v$ can be written as:

$$
Ax = 
\begin{bmatrix}
a_1 & a_2 & ... & a_n
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
x_1 \\
x_2 \\
... \\
x_n
\end{bmatrix} = 
x_1a_1 + x_2a_2 + ... + x_na_n
$$

> Here entries in the form $a_c$ are all vectors

## Properties of matrix vector products

1. $A(u+v) = Au + Av$
2. $A(cu) = cA(u)$
