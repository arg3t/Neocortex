I an adjacency map, each vertice in the [[/notes/Graphs|graph]] is mapped to a list of every [[/notes/Vertice Terminology#^39dda4|incident]] edge. Similar to the [[/notes/Edge List|Edge List]], it uses $O(n+m)$ space.
